What is My Last Wish?

*My Last Wish* is an emotionally gripping, story-driven adventure game by MuseFuze Studios. Set in a dystopian world, the game combines action-packed gameplay with a profound narrative that explores themes of humanity, loss, redemption, and the psychological effects of trauma on behavior and relationships. Dive into the journey of Sophie, a complex protagonist, as she navigates through perilous missions, uncovers secrets about her past, and struggles with the emotional scars of her experiences. The game challenges players' morality, pushing them to make difficult decisions that shape both Sophie's journey and the world around her.

Development Highlights

  • Development Test 1 - Closed

    The first development test introduced a basic survival game mode, allowing testers to experience the core gameplay mechanics, provide feedback, and enjoy the initial feel of the game.

  • Development Test 2 - Closed

    This test featured refined gameplay mechanics, improved maps, and significant bug fixes, enhancing the experience from the first test.

  • Development Test 3 - Closed

    The third test includes numerous improvements and additions:
    - Basic hair movement simulation to add detail to Sophie’s character
    - Improved vaulting mechanics for smoother and more natural interactions.
    - Enhanced weapon gameplay with delayed recoil effects, paving the way for future details like casings and bullet trails.
    - Fluid movement adjustments for a more immersive feel.
    - Sophie's head movement to follow the player’s view direction for added detail.
    - Gamemode adjustments to the infected gamemode.
    - HUD and quality of life improvements
    - Background NPCs to make the world feel alive and populated. (Delayed)
    - Enemy AI that actively seeks cover and engages the player dynamically. (Delayed)
    - Example Chapter development for story testing and feedback. (Delayed)
    - Performance optimizations and significant bug fixes to improve gameplay stability.

  • Development Test 4 – Closed and Scheduled (June 2025)

    The fourth test is set to pack in a wave of new features and upgrades, focusing on gameplay depth and story integration:
    - Introduction of the long-awaited example story chapter for narrative testing and player feedback.
    - Overhauled sliding and parkour systems for smoother flow and responsiveness.
    - New advanced parkour stunts to expand movement creativity.
    - Sneaking mechanics added for stealth gameplay, adding more tactical options.
    - Refined HUD elements for a cleaner, more informative interface.
    - Expanded weapon and character customization systems, giving players more ways to personalize their playstyle.

  • And A Lot More...

    Each development test iterates on player feedback, bringing new features and improvements to ensure the game delivers a polished and engaging experience.
